~~~~~~~~~~~
Recipe Book
~~~~~~~~~~~

The following sections provide a selection of small recipes from the
community. You will find easy helpful hacks for beginners as well as advanced
usecases. If you are employing convenient small hacks or helpers, that make
your day to day work with oemof.solph better, you are invited to add your
small script or ideas to this page. You can contribute via the
`GitHub repository <https://github.com/oemof/oemof-solph>`__. Furthermore, you
we provide and update a collection in the oemof.org blog on
`this page <https://oemof.org/category/showcase/>`__.
